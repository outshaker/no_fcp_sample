IB-HUB801-TB4 Big Power. Small Package. Thunderbolt™ 4

A hub with top marks! This hub is a high-performance accessory that manages three Thunderbolt™ 4 interfaces and even offers a USB 3.2 Gen 2 port for USB Type-A devices. In addition to 85 W of notebook charging power, the hub supports one 8K@30 Hz or two 4K@60 Hz displays.
